Meet The Founder

Vishakha Bangare

With over 12 years of artistry and 5,200+ brides made beautiful, Vishakha Bangare is one of India's most loved bridal makeup artists. Trained in international airbrush and HD techniques, she founded V Shine Makeup Studio with a single mission — make every Indian woman feel celebrated on her most precious day.

From a small home studio to the city's most premium lavender lounge today, the journey has been crafted with discipline, hygiene, premium products and most of all — heart.
