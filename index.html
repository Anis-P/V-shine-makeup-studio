Frequently Asked

Your Questions, Answered

How early should I book my bridal trial?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 months before your wedding. Peak season (Oct–Feb) tends to fill up faster, so earlier is always safer.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes! Vishakha and her team travel pan-India and to select international destinations. Travel and stay are billed at actuals.

Are your products skin-safe and HD-ready?

Absolutely. We only use globally certified premium brands — MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, Huda Beauty, Bobbi Brown, Estée Lauder and more — tested safe for Indian skin.

Do you offer beauty courses for beginners?

Yes — our academy runs short self-grooming workshops and full professional bridal makeup certifications. Visit the Academy page or call us at 9987936131.

What are your studio timings?

We are open all 7 days from 9 AM to 9 PM. Bridal bookings are also accommodated at home or destination on request.
